HMT Juliet T136

HMT Juliet was launched with a pennant of T136 on October 2 1940.

Initially she served off the coast of Britain.

She took part in Operation Torch which was launched on October 22 1942 in convoy KMS A1. Following that operation she took part in Anti Submarine exercises in the mediteranean. The photograph above shows Juliet on the coast of Malta.

Ship Commanders
