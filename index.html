Plassy Shipwreck

Gob na Cora, Inisheer, Aran Islands

Introduction

Why is she there

The Plassy which features on the opening credits of the well-known sitcom, Father Ted, lies shipwrecked on the eastern shore of Inisheer at Gob an Cora close to Tr√° Caroach, the smallest of the Aran Islands archipelago which are in turn located in Galway Bay or Lough Lurgan on the west coast of Ireland.

When did it happen

She ran aground on the Finis rock, currenty marked by a buoy about 1km east of her current location during heavy seas while shipping cargo from Limerick City to Galway City in March 1960.

Where is she from

The Plassy was originally launched in 1940 as HMS Juliet with the designation T136, as an Anti-Submarine Escort and Mine Sweeper. She was subsequently converted to a cargo ship and named Peterjon before finally ending up with the Limerick Steamship Company and remnamed Plassy.
