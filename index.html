Intro

1940

Ordered by the British Royal Navy as an Anti-Submarine Escort and Minesweper, designated T136, HMS Juliet is launched on Oct 2nd 1940.

1947

Acquired by Regent Shippers, London and converted into a cargo vessel. Relaunched in 1947 as the Peterjon.

1951

Acquired by the Limerick Steamship Company in 1951 and renamed as the Plassey.

1960

While delivering cargo from Limerick to Galway on March 8th 1960, the Plassy ran aground on the Finnis Rock, Inisheer.
