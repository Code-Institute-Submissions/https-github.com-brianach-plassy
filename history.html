Origins

The ship was ordered on 12 December 1939. Due to a lack of armed trawler/sweeper/escort vessels, she was ordered to be built as a Shakespearian class trawler which is a subclass of the much larger Isles class. She was built by Cook, Welton & Gemmel, Shipbuilders of Hull and Beverley, 1883 - 1963 with a steam engine supplied by Holmes. Photograph of Welton & Gemmell Shipyard in 1950.
