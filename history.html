Origins

The ship was ordered by the British Royal Navy on 12 December 1939.

She was built as a Shakespearian class trawler which is a subclass of the much larger Isles class.

She was built by Cook, Welton & Gemmel, Shipbuilders of Hull and Beverley, 1883 - 1963 with a steam engine supplied by Charles D Holmes & Co.

Ship Specifications

Builder : Cook, Welton & Gemmel
Engine : Charles D Holmes & Co
Displacement : 545 tonnes
Dimensions : 50m o/a 45.7m pp x 8.45m x 3.35m
Powerplant : Single shaft VTE 950IHP
Speed : 12 knots
Armament : 1 x 12pdr AA 3x20mm AA (3x single)
Complement : 35 crew
